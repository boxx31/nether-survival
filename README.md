# Nether Challenges (1.21.6-26.2)
[Modrinth page](https://modrinth.com/datapack/nether-challenges)
### About
This datapack adds over 50+ new advancements of varying difficulty for a **vanilla Nether-only** survival challenge.

To keep the challenge as vanilla as possible, this pack **does not make gameplay changes** other than experience rewards and **automatically sets the default spawn** in the nether. All attainable in-game advancements are kept, and several have been reworked to account for limited resources in the nether.

This datapack is multiplayer compatible.
### Sample challenges
- Hydroplosive - _Kill three Blazes using a single water bottle splash_
- The Conquerer - _Remove every Chest and Gold Block in a Bastion Remnant_
- Nether Hunted - _Kill one of every hostile monster_
- Jockey Tower - _Get chased a baby Hoglin with three riders_
- Recipe Head - _Discover all crafting recipes_
